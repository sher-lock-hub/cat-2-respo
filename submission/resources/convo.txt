My chosen business operates a small business that specializes in selling jewellery and perfumes. 
Their product range includes earrings, necklaces, hair accessories, and fragrances- 
although perfumes are quite new in their stock so i didn't include it. 
The business aims to provide stylish, high-quality products that help customers express 
their individuality and elegance, hence the brand name Limit.Ed

The primary target audience consists of working-class individuals  but they also cater for other customers.
The client would like visitors to immediately see the available products and understand the brand's identity
so the website should highlight the uniqueness and elegance of the jewellery collection while showcasing featured products.
The preferred colour palette includes white and gold.
